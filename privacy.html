Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 15, 2026  |  App name: Story AI

This Privacy Policy describes how Rasim Eren Karakaş ("we", "us") collects, uses, and protects your personal and usage data in the Story AI mobile application ("App"). By using the App, you accept this policy.

1. Data We Collect

1.1 Device and Identity

  • Anonymous user ID: An anonymous identifier generated on your device for using the App without signing in. This ID is not linked to your email or real name.
  • Device information: Operating system, app version, and similar technical data (for providing the service and troubleshooting).

1.2 Usage Data

  • Story creation count and timestamps: For daily limits, premium cooldown, and service improvement.
  • Subscription status: Whether you are a premium (paid) or free user; used for subscription management and applying limits.

1.3 Content Data

  • Story parameters: Age range, category, language, reading duration, story style, and (for premium users) character names and location. This data is used for story generation and may be processed on our servers; it is not permanently tied to a personal profile.

1.4 Payment and Subscription

  • Subscription and payment: Handled via Adapty and the store (App Store / Google Play). We do not store credit card or payment details on our servers; such data is subject to Adapty and store policies.

1.5 Advertising

  • Ad delivery: We use Google Mobile Ads to show ads to free users. Ad identifiers and ad interactions are subject to Google's privacy policy.

2. How We Use Data

  • To provide story generation and other app features
  • To enforce daily usage limits and premium cooldown rules
  • To manage subscription and payment (via Adapty and the store)
  • To show ads (Google Mobile Ads)
  • To improve service quality and security
  • To comply with legal obligations

3. Third Parties and Data Sharing

  • OpenAI: Text and image prompts are processed for story and image generation. OpenAI's data use policy applies.
  • Adapty: Subscription and payment information is processed; Adapty's privacy policy applies.
  • Google (Mobile Ads): Ad delivery and ad identifiers; Google's privacy policy applies.
  • App Store / Google Play: Subscription and payment are subject to store policies.

These parties process data under their own policies; we only share what is necessary to provide the service.

4. Data Storage and Security

  • Data is processed through our infrastructure and third-party providers (OpenAI, Adapty, Google, etc.); their own data processing and storage policies apply.
  • Anonymous IDs and usage data are retained as long as needed for providing the service and legal retention.
  • We do not store payment information on our servers.
  • We use secure means for communication and server access.

5. Children's Privacy

Story AI offers content aimed at children (e.g. age ranges 3–5, 6–8). The App should be used by users under 13 only under parental or guardian supervision. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13; the data we collect is limited to anonymous identifiers and usage data.

6. Your Rights

  • Access / correction: You may request information about the data we hold about you.
  • Deletion: You may request deletion of anonymous ID and usage data; you can clear app data from your device or contact us.
  • Objection / restriction: You may object to or request restriction of processing where the law allows.
  • Complaint: You may lodge a complaint with a data protection authority under applicable law (e.g. GDPR).
